Troubleshooting
  • Make sure the ESP32 is advertising BLE and your computer/phone Bluetooth is ON.
  • Web Bluetooth works best on desktop Chrome/Edge. iOS Safari does not support Web Bluetooth.
  • Your ESP32 must expose the Service UUID + Notify Characteristic UUID that match demo.js.
