My BackPal

A smart backpack system that helps students monitor backpack load and reduce the risk of back and posture-related injuries.

Problem

Many students carry backpacks that are too heavy, which can cause back, neck, and shoulder pain over time.

Solution

My BackPal uses a load sensor inside a backpack connected to an ESP32 to monitor weight and alert users when the load becomes unsafe.
